TOP 10 BEST FOOD IN MALAYSIA

1. Nasi Lemak

Nasi Lemak is Malaysia's national dish, featuring fragrant rice cooked in coconut milk, served with sambal, boiled eggs, peanuts, and anchovies. This dish is a beloved breakfast staple and a must-try for anyone visiting Malaysia.

2. Char Kway Teow

This stir-fried flat noodle dish is rich in flavor, cooked with soy sauce, prawns, cockles, eggs, and bean sprouts. It’s a popular street food that captures the essence of Malaysian cuisine.

3. Laksa

A spicy noodle soup with a tangy, savory broth made from tamarind or coconut milk, served with seafood or chicken. It’s a dish full of bold flavors and is one of Malaysia’s most iconic soups.

4. Roti Canai

A crispy, flaky flatbread served with dhal, curry, or condensed milk. It’s a beloved breakfast item in Malaysia, perfect for any time of the day.

5. Satay

Grilled skewered meat served with a delicious peanut sauce, perfect for gatherings and celebrations. Satay is a favorite street food snack across the country.

6. Rendang

A rich and tender dry curry made with beef or chicken, slow-cooked in coconut milk and spices. The depth of flavor in rendang makes it a beloved dish for many.

7. Hainanese Chicken Rice

Poached chicken served with fragrant rice, chili sauce, and soy sauce. Simple yet incredibly flavorful, Hainanese chicken rice is a comforting dish enjoyed by all ages.

8. Cendol

A refreshing dessert made with shaved ice, palm sugar syrup, coconut milk, and green jelly noodles. Cendol is a perfect way to cool off in Malaysia’s tropical heat.

9. Nasi Kerabu

A blue rice dish colored with butterfly pea flowers, served with fish, pickles, and a spicy coconut-based sauce. Nasi Kerabu is a visually stunning and flavorful dish.

10. Apam Balik

Known as a Malaysian pancake, Apam Balik is filled with crushed peanuts, corn, and sugar. It’s a sweet and crunchy treat often enjoyed as a street snack.
